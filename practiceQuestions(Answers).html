outliers = ((titanic1 < lowerBound) | (titanic1 > upperBound)).any(axis=1)

Scatter plots highlight the relationships between continuous variables, identifying outliers as data points deviationg significantly from the entire pattern, which assists in the provision of valuable insight into correlation between variables. Based on where they are in relation to other data points, scatter plots can be used to identify outliers. By observing points that are far from the primary cluster, it is possible to find outliers in the range of "age" to "fare".
